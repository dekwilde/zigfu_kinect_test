Warning

This demo app is based on a web-browser!
Don't expect usual performance you know from other VIP apps.

How to use

  1. Position yourself, so that you are recognized by the sensor.
  2. Raise your hand in front of your body - wait until cursor appears.
  3. Then move your hand to move the cursor.
  4. Move the cursor onto a content-button and wait until it turns violet and then 3secs to select the button.
  5. If the cursor disappears, restart from step 2 to reset the hand-session.
